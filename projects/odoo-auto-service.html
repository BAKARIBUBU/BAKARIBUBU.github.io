Odoo Auto Service System

This project is a comprehensive custom solution built on Odoo 17, designed to streamline the entire automotive service lifecycle. It transforms sales orders into full-fledged workshop orders with a smart button, automating vehicle check-in and seamlessly replicating inspection checklists into the dispatch phase. The system integrates Odoo modules such as Sales, Fleet, and Accounting to provide a unified workflow. Key features include:

  • Automated conversion of sales orders to workshop orders.
  • Seamless transfer of vehicle checklists from check-in to dispatch.
  • Dynamic PDF reporting with formatted check-in/out times and detailed service data.
  • Integration with partner modules for enhanced data consistency.
  • Robust error handling and security access for reliable operations.

Built using Python, XML, and QWeb, this ERP customization not only improves workflow efficiency but also enhances customer communication by providing detailed, professional repair orders and service history reports. The project demonstrates advanced expertise in Odoo development and process automation, marking a significant improvement in auto service management.
